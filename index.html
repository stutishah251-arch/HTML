Sustainable Development Goal 14

Goal 14: Life Below Water

Conserve and Sustainably Use the Oceans

SDG 14 focuses on conserving and sustainably using the oceans, seas and marine resources. It aims to reduce marine pollution, protect ecosystems, regulate fishing, prevent ocean acidification, and support actions that keep our oceans healthy for future generations.

Key Focus Areas

Marine Biodiversity

Oceans are home to millions of species. Protecting habitats like coral reefs and seagrass beds keeps this biodiversity alive.

Pollution Control

Reducing plastic waste, chemicals and oil spills is essential to prevent damage to marine ecosystems.

Conservation Actions

Beach clean-ups, marine protected areas and responsible fishing help restore and conserve ocean life.
